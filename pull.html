PULL

Unreal Engine Project

Overview

Pull is a third-person action/puzzle-platformer game that narratively takes place in a fantasy dream settings, filled with abundantly clever puzzle-solving sequences, ‘cartoonish-like’ visual aesthetics and compelling narrative storyline. Distinct elements from other puzzle-platformer (i.e. environment-based interactions) and action (i.e. ability-based gameplay) game genres inspired the development of the game, however providing a slightly different approach to provide a unique gameplay experience. It intends to mainly attract puzzle and/or platform gamers, as well as action gamers who enjoy dynamic gameplay mechanics. There is no specific gender and domain targeted, thus making the game easily accessible and have all gamer types open to enjoy the unique gameplay mechanics.
